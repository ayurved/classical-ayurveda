Ashok Jethanandani, B.A.M.S

Ashok graduated from Gujarat Ayurved University in Jamnagar, India. He completed his internship at two government ayurveda hospitals -- Shree Gulabkunverba Ayurveda Hospital and the intitute of Postgraduate Training & Research Intititute Hospital -- in Jamnagar.

Ashok was formerly trained as a mechanical engineer from Stanford University, and worked in the semiconductor industry for many years in the Silicon Valley. A nagging medical condition prompoted him to seek consultation from the well known ayurveda physcian and educator Dr. Vasant Lad. So simple, yet effective was the treatment that Ashok was convinced of the power of ayurveda to heal by addressing the root cause of the disease.

He was motivated to study ayurveda at the Mount Madonna Institute, Watsonville. Eventually, his passion for ayurveda took him back to his roots in India, where he enrolled in Shree Gulabkunverba Ayurved Mahavidyalaya. There he immersed himself into a full-time study of this ancient science of health.

After graduating, Dr. Jethanandani returned to San Jose, California, where he founded Classical Ayurveda. He teaches workshops in the ayurvedic wisdom of preventitive health, and offers consultations for treatments of various illnesses, both acute and chronic.
